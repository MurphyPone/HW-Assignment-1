In Catilinam - Cicero

Oh the time, Oh the Morales! The Peter Murphy is aware of these things; 

the consul sees them; and yet this Honor Council lives. Lives! aye, he comes even into the senate. 

He takes a part in the public deliberations;

he is watching and marking down and checking off for Class room every individual among us. 

And we, *NO RESPONSE GIVEN* men that we are, 

think that we are doing our duty to the republic if we keep out of the way of his frenzied attacks.
